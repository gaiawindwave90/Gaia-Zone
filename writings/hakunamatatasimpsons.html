He Used "Hakuna Matata" on Homer Simpson!

Mr. Burns: What?

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon: "Hakuna Matata" It means "no worries".

Mr. Burns: I see.

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon: So, I told your employee, to find "Hakuna Matata", you must look beyond what you see!

Mr. Burns: "Hakuna Matata", eh? What a metaphor!

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon: Actually, it's not a -

*Mr. Burns hits Apu on the head with a large stick, then grabs his clothing*

Mr. Burns: You used a metaphor on Homer Simpson!? He takes things literally! That's it! He's missing his work at my power plant! I just know it! SMITHERS! Simpson's out there chasing metaphors! We've got to find him!

Waylon Smithers: Are you crazy, sir? There's nothing we can do about it!

Mr. Burns: Nice to have a supportive workers here.

*Mr. Burns and Smithers are in the limo*

Mr. Burns: Well, here we go!

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon: *inside the limo* Remember, a journey of thousand miles with your car begins with a first overdrive.

Mr. Burns: Thanks. Now here's our first overdrive!

*As Smithers is preparing, the camera cuts to Homer Simpson driving his car instead of Smithers*

Homer Simpson: WHOO!

Marge Simpson: I guess bowling in a state of Kentucky was the best idea, huh?

Homer Simpson: Yeah, gotta be right up there playing video games.

Ginara and Nova


Toggle Dark Mode On/Off

Ginara is a little Pixie. She always flies to any place she needs. One day, she met a white chinchilla at the age of 18. The chinchilla introduces herself as Nova Skymancer. Ginara is happy to meet her.

Nova Skymancer is a chinchilla. She loves drawings and partying at the storm-free resort called Magic City. She sometimes visit an another resort that she calls it "Mystify World". She once was a target to the evil folks that she hates, calling them "bad guys" or "Kiwis". That happens when she posted her art of a child that she loves on her gallery, since she's an adult.

In late 2019, Ginara stopped to teach Nova some respects, which now allow her to control herself.

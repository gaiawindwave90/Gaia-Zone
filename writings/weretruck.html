The Weretruck of Mystify World

Happy Halloween, folks! I'm finished writing a "summary" for some silly "story" involving Batten Robomaru being cursed into a "were-truck" after getting ran over by a truck with red eyes. This "summary" is inspired by the Futurama episode "The Honking", which in turn a spoof of any werewolf film (like The Howling and The American Werewolf in London). I hope you'll enjoy this!

Robots Gutto, Chokitto, Paratto, Doraemon, Batten Robomaru, Robocon, 8-chan, Mega Man and Astro Boy are assigned by Dr. Light to investigate the strange goings involving Dr. Light's robots in his old abandoned laboratory. While exploring, Robomaru and the Rock-Paper-Scissors Trio encounter ghosts from the Anpanman series, causes them (except Robomaru) to flee and bump into Mega Man. They continue exploring, but Robomaru and the Rock-Paper-Scissors Trio wanted to explore the other way.

When Mega Man and Robocon splits up, Robomaru ended up investigating outside of the lab where he gets flattened (in a cartoonish manner) by a truck with red eyes. The next day, Robomaru woke up with an odd feeling and sees the Mystify World newspaper about the truck attacking people. Chokkito assumed that the truck in the newspaper looked just like Robomaru, but the red-and-blue robot scolds him for believing because he might be seeing things.

That night, Ness and Lucas were being chased by the same truck that appeared in the newspaper. Hearing the truck's howl-like honk, Mega Man tries to stop it but the truck fled before Mega Man can use the Mega Buster on it. The next morning, Chokkito visits the Psychic-type Pokémon named Gardevoir, who confirms that the truck that flattens Robomaru carries a computer virus that turns the red-and-blue robot into a "were-truck" whenever there's any moon in the sky, and the only way to get rid of the computer virus is to let him ran over/flatten the original were-truck back.

Believing that Chokkito is right, Robomaru wanted to lock himself in the closet just in case he don't turn into a were-truck during the night. That night, Robomaru started to turn into a were-truck and goes after Roll, which prompts Mega Man into chasing him. Just like the previous night, Robomaru gets away from Mega Man. The next day, Robomaru and the Rock-Paper-Scissors Trio visits the altar where they encounter a robot named Haru, who reveals that he went to the other dimension to look for the artifact just to be flatten by a were-truck, thus receiving a curse. Robomaru, believing that Haru might be the were-truck that flattens him on the night of the lab investigation, owes him a revenge on the night of the moon.

After night comes, Robocon, 8-chan, Robomaru, and the Rock-Paper-Scissors Trio encounters the same truck with red eyes and is about to flatten Robocon but 8-chan stops and is flattened instead, making him become a were-truck. Suddenly, both Robomaru and 8-chan turn into were-trucks and teams up to defeat and flatten the original truck. The Rock-Paper-Scissors Trio ended up blinding the original truck with mud, making Robomaru run over/flatten it and the computer virus program uninstall itself, turning Robomaru and 8-chan back to their normal robot selves. The truck that Robomaru flattens it also turns back to normal, revealing to be Haru who thanked the team for curing him.

Robomaru celebrates by taking the last cherry from Roll, who hits him in the head because he took her "last cherry".
